Hunt Your Own National Treasure

  • Tech Stack: Javascript, HTML5, CSS3
  • Project URL: natl-treasure-cyoadventure.netlify.app
  • GitHub Repository: github.com/natnichols/cyo-natl-treasure

Hunt Your Own National Treasure is a Choose Your Own Adventure Game inspired by the cinematic masterpiece National Treasure (2004). Designed and built in 2 weeks with the goal of taking the player on their own personalized journey through the plotlines of National Treasure.
